Ferrari

A car manufacturer of classic Italian sports cars based in Maranello, Italy

Ferrari is famous for its extraordinary speeds, refined luxury, and for being the builders of the most expensive car ever sold, making it one of the most recognizable names in the world

Origins

Ferrari, an Italian company known for its sports cars, traces its roots back to 1929 when Enzo Ferrari formed the Scuderia Ferrari racing team.

Scuderia Ferrari

The oldest and most successful team in Formula One history has won 16 Constructors’ Championships and 15 Drivers’ Championships, accumulating more race victories, podiums, pole positions, fastest laps, and points than any other team.
